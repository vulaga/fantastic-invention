Who I Am

I'm Vukašin Đorđević, a graphic designer and motion artist from Serbia. Under the alias VuxDZN, I create visual identities, motion graphics and social media content that cut through the noise.

My focus is on gaming brands, online creators and businesses that want to look sharp and be taken seriously. I don't do generic, every project is built from scratch around what the brand actually needs.

Design, for me, is about making things feel inevitable, like the brand couldn't have looked any other way.
